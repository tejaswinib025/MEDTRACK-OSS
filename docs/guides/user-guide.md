# MedTrack OSS User Guide

## Getting Started
1. **Sign Up**: Create an account with your email.
2. **Add Medication**: Click "Add Medication" and enter the name, dosage, and frequency.
3. **Set Reminders**: Choose how you want to be notified (Email, SMS, or Push).
4. **Log Adherence**: When you take your medication, mark it as "Taken" in the app.

## Dashboard Overview
- **Upcoming**: View your schedule for the next 24 hours.
- **History**: Review your adherence trends over the last 30 days.
- **Caregivers**: Manage who can see your health data.
